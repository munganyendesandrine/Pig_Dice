Pig Dice Game

Rules

  1. This game of Pig Dice is best with two players
  2. The dice has 6 sides, each one contains 1 circle,2 circles,3 circles,4 circles,5 circles,6 circles respectively.
  3. The first player rolls the die. If he rolls as 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6, he writes down the score.
  4. If he did not roll a 1, the player can decide to roll again. If he again rolls a 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6, he adds that number to his previous score.
  5. If he rolls a 1, he earns no score and immediately passes the die to the next player.
  6. However, If the player rolls a 1, he loses all his points (for that turn only) and must pass the die to the next player.
  7. when a player scores 100 points wil be the winner of the game
